Ayako and Caroline went to the Slow-to-Fast Earthquakes workshop in Nara (Japan), an international workshop organized by Tokyo University to gather scientists specialised in Fast and Slow Earthquakes. Ayako presented a poster about Intermediate-depth earthquakes and Caroline gave a 15-minutes talk about Slow slip events in Mexico. Liam, Roos and Yudong, graduate students of Camilla Cattania's Fault Mechanics group, also attended this meeting and presented posters.
