Ayako, Jared and Caroline went to the Seismology Student Workshop at Lamont-Doherty Earth Observatory (Colombia University, USA), a 2-days workshop organized by and for graduate students. href="https://eqsci.mit.edu/tecto/author/ayako-tsuchiyama/">Ayako presented a poster about Intermediate-depth earthquakes and Jared gave a talk about volcanoes and slow slip events focused on Mount St. Helens. Liam, Roos and Yudong, graduate students of Camilla Cattania's Fault Mechanics group, also attended this workshop and presented posters.
